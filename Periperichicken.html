Periperi Chicken

Description

Periperi chicken is a flavorful meal of any cut chicken that is marinated in and also served with a homemade peri peri sauce , a hot pepper sauce from South Africa
This recipe can be made with any cut of chicken,though .Pick your favorite part bone-in or boneless chicken breast,chicken thighs, wings you name it and mix it
up with periperi sauce.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken legs
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step by Step Procedure

  1. 1 pound red chillies chopped - African Bird's Eye peppers are traditional , but you can sub with red peppers available to you
  2. 4 cloves garlic chopped
  3. 1 teaspoon smoked paprika you can sub in other chilli powders
  4. 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  5. 1/4 cup chopped basil
  6. 1/2 cup olive oil or vegetable oil
  7. Juice from 1 lemon
  8. Salt to taste(and black pepper , if desired)
