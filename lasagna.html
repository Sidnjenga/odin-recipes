Lasagna

Description

This is an easy to make homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from scratch tomato sauce and a delicious cheese filling with some ricotta.
Additionally you can either use small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan ,you can't go wrong with either.Finally serve with a leafy green salad and
crusty garlic bread

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • diced onions
  • A can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two tablespoons of parsley and a clove of garlic
  • A dash of sugar
  • Dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles(preferably oven-ready)
  • Eggs

Step by Step Procedure

  1. Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly
  2. Add the onion and cook until it's translucent
  3. Stir in the canned tomato products , half of the parsley ,garlic,basil,1.5 teaspoons of salt,oregano and sugar
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente
  5. Mis cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese,eggs ,the reamaining parsley, the remaining salt and pepper in a bowl
  6. Layer the ingredients according to the recipe(starting with the sauce and end with the mozzarella cheese and fully assembled)
  7. Cover with the foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour
  8. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown
