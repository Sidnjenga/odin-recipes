Smoked Salmon Sushi Roll

Description

This Homemade Salmon roll made with sushi rice,nori,and smoked salmon
This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Wasabi paste
  • Smoked Salmon
  • A Cucumber
  • An Avocado

Step by Step Procedure

  1. Soak and cook the rice , then mix in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate
  2. Assemble the sushi rolls
  3. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces
